Researching Wiggly Paint

By Kelly Xiao

Research Questions

Q1.

WigglyPaint by Internet Janitor is a creative digital painter software that is experimental and abstract.

Q2.

Three other softwares in the digital painting genre include Krita, Adobe photoshop and MS Paint.

Q3.

WigglyPaint has many interactive features/mechanics, but the main ones I have interacted with includes:

  • Wiggly Brushes: There are multiple fun textured brushes and highlighter that create wiggly animations when painted with.
  • Sound effects: Each brush when utilised prodice a quirky wobbly sound that match the brushstrokes.
  • Erasing and undo: The "OBLITERATE!" feature at the right bottom corner allows the user to explode the canvas, erasing everything while the "OOPS!" button undo the previous action.
  • Beyond the canvas: The software gives users the option to creatively paint and move around widgets outside the canvas by clicking tool in the menu bar.

Q4.

Brush sizes, multiple brush and shape options, colour manipulation, selection/lasso tool, crop, undo and obliteration of the canvas are all features/mechanics of WigglyPaint that match digital painting software conventions as they are commonly included and used in them. Therefore, indicating its genre to be a digital painter.

Q5.

The novel features/mechanics that set WigglyPaint apart from traditional digital painting software include:

  • The wiggly brushstrokes: WigglyPaint as the name states, creates fluid strokes that constantly wiggles as the user paints unlike traditional digital painting tools.
  • Sound effects: Brushes with its own sound effects that play when painted with gives a more fun immersive experience different from the typical paint softwares.
  • Widget interactions: Users are unrestricted from just painting, they can also move the brushes and canvas around, which creates a playful and experimental envirionment.

Q6.

Layering systems are a common feature in digital painting softwares that is missing in WigglyPaint. While adding a new canvas is possible, there isn't a display for seeing mulitple layers. Another feature that can be added is canvas rotation and zoom, so users can focus on an area better while painting.

Q7.

WigglyPaint is primary aimed at artists and creative enthusiasts who enjoy fun casual painting and want to explore with experimentally abstract digital paint softwares. It has a playful and simplistic design, and the wiggly brush mechanic encourages creative exploration without the pressured feeling of producing professional polished work in a more complex digital painting program.

Q8.

What i found annoying when interacting with WigglyPaint included how the marker size feature doesn't work when adjusting the brush sizes, as well as being lost on a blank white screen after clicking new deck or new card, not knowing how to get back.

Q9.

The most enjoyable interaction with WigglyPaint was the main wiggly feature with the sound effects. Creating an illustration with it being animated wiggly is fun and different from the typical digital paint softwares that i use.
